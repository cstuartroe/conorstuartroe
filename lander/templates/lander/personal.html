Personal Stuff

bio

I originally hail from Asheville, NC, USA, a funky little town situated in the heart of Appalachia. I lived there until the age of 16, when I left home for bigger and better things, namely to attend the NC School of Science and Mathematics, a residential public magnet high school in Durham, NC.

After graduating in 2015 I took a year off of school to travel and work, which is when I filled out most of the travel section below, and a handy chunk of the languages. In July 2015 I competed in the International Olympiad in Linguistics, where I won a silver medal. Since August 2016 I've attended Haverford College, a very small liberal arts college in Philadelphia.
